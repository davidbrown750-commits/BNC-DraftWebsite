13. Concluding Remarks

A short word to close the book and to point you toward where radar goes next.

13.1 Wrap Up

The field of radar studies encompasses many disciplines. Radar systems are widely deployed and address many applications in society. The field continues to grow with multichannel and multi-frequency systems that test the limits of today's electronics and circuitry. While this exciting field offers careers at all stages, we hope this overview of the technology has familiarized you with the basic building blocks of all radar technology.
