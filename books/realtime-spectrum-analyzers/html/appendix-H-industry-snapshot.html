Appendix H

Industry Snapshot: Who Hires for Spectrum Skills

The "where do I work" companion to Chapter 13's "what jobs exist." A map of the employers, the hiring trends, and the salary bands for spectrum work in 2026.

Test and Measurement OEMs

  • Aaronia AG (Germany) - SPECTRAN V6 PLUS family, IsoLOG 3D DF, RTSA Suite PRO. Strong in counter-UAS and EW.
  • Berkeley Nucleonics Corporation (BNC) (US) - RTSA distribution and integration.
  • Keysight Technologies (US) - UXA, PXA, EXA spectrum analyzers; broad RF test portfolio.
  • Rohde & Schwarz (Germany) - FSWP, FSW, FPL series; strong in cellular test.
  • Anritsu (Japan) - MS2840A, MS2090A series; strong in field analyzers.
  • Tektronix (US) - RSA series RTSAs; oscilloscope-RTSA convergence.
  • Signal Hound (US) - Compact USB analyzers; cost-effective tier.
  • ThinkRF (Canada) - R5500 distributed sensors for spectrum monitoring.
  • Per Vices (Canada) - High-performance research-grade SDR/RTSAs.

Software-Defined Radio

  • Ettus Research / NI (US) - USRP family, the standard for research SDR.
  • Nuand (US) - bladeRF series.
  • Great Scott Gadgets (US) - HackRF One, Ubertooth.
  • RFspace (US) - Compact networked SDRs.
  • Lime Microsystems (UK) - LimeSDR family.
  • Microsoft Azure RTOS, AWS Wavelength - Cloud-edge SDR platforms.

Hiring Trends (2026)

  • Counter-UAS: roles up sharply; 30-50 percent year-over-year growth at major systems integrators.
  • Cellular RF field engineering: in demand for 5G dense-urban deployments.
  • EW analysts: in shortage globally; clearance-required positions especially scarce.
  • RF cybersecurity: emerging as a discrete job family with premium salaries.
  • AI-for-RF research: concentrated in academic-industry consortia and select R&D groups at primes.
  • 6G research: growing as the standardization timeline accelerates.
